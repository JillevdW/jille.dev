About the team

We're a group of friends with a passion for gaming. Beat that!

We're a group of friends consisting of computer engineers, game developers and an artist, and we try to make cool games every now and then.

"Being able to shape an idea into a game with proper use of art, sounds and storybuilding, that is magical."

The youth is the future.

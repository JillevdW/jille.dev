Personal lessons

With as subject things like Photoshop, computer usage or how to use your phones and social media. Personal, in a place where you feel comfortable.

A year ago I started giving Photoshop lessons to some friends. After receiving a lot of positive feedback, I thought it'd be amazing to try and start a company out of it. Today I give lessons in a broader range of subjects.

My goal with the lessons is to enhance your experience with technology, and to make the usage more pleasant, for example by explaining how certain functions of your computer or phone work, and how you can implement those in your daily life. If you're looking for something in particular, you can send me a message and I'll see what I can do.

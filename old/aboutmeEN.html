About me

My name is Jille van der Weerd, an 19-year old HBO-ICT student.

I've always had something with computers. When I was nine years old, I started writing code, and I hope make my living with that in the future. I just think it has something magical, making whatever you want without limit. Whether that's an application that listens to your voice and executes your tasks, or a game that you're really inside of. I just think it's beautiful.

And how amazing would it be, with a hobby like that, to take the leap and try to start your own company? Well that is what I'm doing right now. With this I hope to help people with technology, so they can use it the way it is meant to be used, nice and comfortable.
